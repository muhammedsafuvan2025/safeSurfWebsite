Advanced NLP Pattern Recognition

Our extension uses Natural Language Processing to automatically detect privacy policy pages as you browse websites. When identified, it extracts the policy text for analysis.

OpenAI API Integration

We leverage OpenAI's powerful language models through their API to scan the policy text, identify concerning patterns, and generate user-friendly summaries of complex legal language.

Risk Classification System

Our proprietary algorithm categorizes privacy risks into severity levels, flagging terms related to data collection, sharing, and retention that could impact your privacy.

Real-time Processing

All analysis happens in real-time with minimal latency, providing instant insights as you browse, with your data privacy preserved throughout the process.
